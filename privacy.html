Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use ASPD Forum, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Username and password (encrypted) when you register
  • Content: Posts, threads, messages, and other content you create
  • Usage Data: IP addresses (hashed for privacy), timestamps, and interaction data
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type for optimizing your experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the forum service
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Prevent spam, abuse, and enforce community guidelines
  • Improve the user experience
  • Send important service-related notifications

3. Data Protection

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt encryption
  • IP addresses are hashed before storage
  • JWT tokens are used for secure authentication
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent abuse

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Update your profile information
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies and local storage to:

  • Keep you logged in (JWT tokens)
  • Remember your preferences
  • Maintain session state

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, we remove personal data within 30 days, though some anonymized data may be retained for security and analytics purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

ASPD Forum is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through the forum or via notification.

10. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact the forum administrators through the platform.
