this isnt a heavily moderated space. were not going to police your tone or require you to be nice. but there are a few things that will get you banned because they make the forum useless or create legal problems.

read this once. its short.

[01] THE BASICS

NO DOXXING

dont post anyones real identity, location, workplace, or other identifying information. this includes your own if youre trying to prove a point. anonymity protects everyone here. break this and youre gone immediately, no warnings.

NO EXPLICIT ILLEGAL PLANNING

discussing past experiences is fine. asking for advice on active criminal situations is fine. but dont use the forum to plan or coordinate illegal activities. we dont want to give law enforcement reasons to shut this down.

NO MINORS

you must be 18+ to use this forum. we discuss adult topics and dont want to deal with the legal implications of minors participating. if youre under 18, come back later.

NO SPAM OR COMMERCIAL SHIT

dont advertise your business, promote your youtube channel, or drop affiliate links. this isnt the place. one warning, then ban.

[02] WHAT IS AND ISNT ALLOWED

✓ ALLOWED

  • blunt, direct communication - no need to soften things
  • discussing manipulation, lack of empathy, impulsivity openly
  • talking about past legal issues or criminal history
  • dark humor - we get it
  • disagreeing with people, even harshly
  • discussing controversial coping mechanisms
  • venting about neurotypical expectations
  • asking for practical advice on difficult situations
  • questioning your diagnosis or others experiences

✗ NOT ALLOWED

  • posting identifying information about anyone
  • coordinating illegal activities
  • targeting specific individuals for harassment
  • posting content involving minors in any harmful context
  • spam, advertising, or self-promotion
  • intentionally disrupting discussions with no purpose
  • creating multiple accounts to evade bans

[03] COMMUNITY EXPECTATIONS

DONT FAKE IT

if you dont have ASPD, youre still welcome to lurk or participate, but dont pretend you do. researchers, family members, curious people - fine. just be honest about where youre coming from. we can usually tell anyway.

DONT PLAY THERAPIST

share your experience, not medical advice. "this worked for me" is fine. "you should definitely do X" when youre not a professional isnt helpful and could be harmful. we all hate unsolicited advice - dont become what you hate.

CONTRIBUTE SOMETHING

if youre going to post, say something worth reading. low-effort trolling, constant negativity with no substance, or just being annoying for the sake of it makes the forum worse for everyone. theres a difference between being blunt and being useless.

USE THE RIGHT ROOM

we have different rooms for different topics. relationship stuff goes in relationships, legal questions go in legal, etc. it keeps things organized and makes the forum more useful for everyone searching for specific topics.

HOW WE HANDLE VIOLATIONS

minor stuff: warning, then temp ban, then permanent ban. major stuff (doxxing, illegal coordination, anything involving minors): immediate permanent ban, no appeals. we dont have time for drama or lengthy debates about moderation decisions. if you get banned and think it was wrong, you can email but dont expect much. the forum works because we keep it clean of the stuff that would destroy it.
